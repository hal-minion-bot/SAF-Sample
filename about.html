Our Story

Ian Ritchie has spent over two decades working directly with Canadian seniors and their families. He's sat at kitchen tables in Ontario, navigated Ontario Health at Home applications, helped families through hospital discharges with nowhere to turn, and watched families struggle with the same fragmented system over and over.

What he discovered: the system isn't broken because of bad people. It's fragmented because seniors and families don't have a guide. Discharge planners, social workers, and care coordinators do their best, but they're overbooked. Home care assessors work with strict criteria. There's nobody whose job is to make sure you understand the whole picture and your real options.

Seniors Aging Forward exists to fill that gap. We systematize real-world expertise and make it available to every Canadian family — not just those lucky enough to know someone who's navigated this before.
