Common Questions

How long does the response take?

We aim to respond within 48 hours during business days. For urgent situations (like an imminent hospital discharge), reach out directly and mention "urgent" in your message.

Are you a healthcare provider?

No. We're a navigation and education platform. We don't provide medical advice. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

What if I'm in an emergency right now?

If someone is experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, loss of consciousness, severe injury, or suicidal thoughts, call 911 immediately. We can help after the emergency is stabilized.

Will my information be shared?

Never. Your information is never shared, sold, or used for marketing. We're PIPEDA-compliant and treat your privacy as non-negotiable.

Can I update my situation later?

Yes. You can reply to our response email anytime with updates, new concerns, or follow-up questions. We're here to support your journey.
