Frequently Asked Questions

Is this medical advice?

No. Seniors Aging Forward is a navigation and education platform. We help you understand your options, know the right questions to ask, and identify your next steps. We never provide medical diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical instructions. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Which provinces do you cover?

We launched with deep Ontario-specific content (Ontario Health at Home, OHIP, community programs) and are actively building guides for British Columbia and Alberta. All guides flag province-specific differences. Quebec content in French is planned for a future phase.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. Annual plans are refunded on a pro-rated basis within 30 days of purchase.

Does my employer offer this?

Seniors Aging Forward is available to organizations through our B2B program — including group benefits providers, hospital networks, and employers offering caregiver support benefits. Contact us to discuss organizational access.

Is my information private?

Yes. We are fully PIPEDA-compliant. Your personal information and situation details are never sold or shared with third parties. We use your information only to provide personalized navigation guidance and improve our platform.
